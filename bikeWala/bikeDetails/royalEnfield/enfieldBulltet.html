Royal Enfield Bullet 350 Summary

Key Highlights

  • Street bike available in 3 variants and 5 colours
  • Price starts at Rs. 1,73,562 in India
  • Powered by 349cc BS6 engine with 20.2 bhp and 27 Nm torque
  • Front disc and rear drum brakes with anti-locking braking system
  • Weighs 195 kg with a fuel tank capacity of 13 liters

Overview

The Royal Enfield Bullet 350 has received a major update in the 2023 model and is now based on the same J-platform as the Classic and Hunter 350. Royal Enfield has retained the iconic design elements of the Bullet, including the squared-off rear end, round headlamp, chrome components, single-piece seat, and tank design.

Engine and Performance

The latest Bullet 350 is powered by a 349cc, single-cylinder engine that offers 20.2 bhp at 6,100rpm and 27Nm of peak torque at 4,000rpm. It is linked to a five-speed gearbox. The bike rides on telescopic front forks and dual rear shocks, providing a comfortable and stable ride.

Features

The Bullet 350 is minimalistic on the feature front, with bulb illumination throughout. It comes with a semi-digital instrument cluster, which includes an analogue console and a digital inset for the fuel level readout, trip meter, and more. However, it misses out on the tripper navigation feature.

Variants and Colors

Royal Enfield offers the Bullet 350 in three variants and a choice of five color options.
