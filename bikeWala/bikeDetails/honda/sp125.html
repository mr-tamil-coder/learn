Honda SP 125 Summary

Overview

Honda SP 125 is a mileage bike available in 3 variants and 7 colours. SP 125 price starts at Rs. 86,753 in India with top variant price starting from Rs. 91,293. The Honda SP 125 is powered by a 124cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 10.72 bhp and a torque of 10.9 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Honda SP 125 comes up with a combined braking system of both wheels. This SP 125 bike weighs 116 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 11.2 liters.

Latest Features

  • Onboard diagnostics system for real-time emissions tracking.
  • 123.94cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled engine.
  • Delivers 10.7bhp at 7,500rpm and 10.9Nm of peak torque at 6,000rpm.
  • Five-speed gearbox.

Styling

The styling cues retain the single-pod headlight, body-coloured headlight cowl, body-coloured front fender, fuel tank shrouds, a body-coloured pillion grabrail, and a side-slung exhaust with a chrome heat shield.

Features

  • LED headlight.
  • Fully-digital instrument cluster.
  • Piston-cooling jet technology for reducing friction and maintaining optimum engine temperature.
  • Engine start/stop switch.
  • Combined braking system.

Competition

The Honda SP125 competes in the premium commuter segment of the Indian market and rivals the likes of the TVS Raider 125 and Hero Glamour here.
